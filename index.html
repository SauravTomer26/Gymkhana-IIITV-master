About Student Gymkhana

Students are life and soul of any educational institution. At IIIT Vadodara, students gets unique opportunities of not just witnessing an institution in the making but participating in the making as well. Winston Churchill once said that we shape our Institution and these in turn shape our lives.

Student Gymkhana is a combined term for all the students' activities other than academics. It consisted of 4 committees i.e. Cultural, Literary, Sports & Technical. Under these committees there are different clubs functioning. Every committee/club has their own Secretary/Head and Joint Secretary which are elected in a democratic way by the members of respective clubs.

About Student Gymkhana

IIIT Vadodara Student-Gymkhana aims at maintaining and providing good extra co-curricular activities to it's students ensuring a perfect balance between their academics & social life through various events conducted by its Clubs headed by different Committees. IIIT Vadodara Student Gymkhana consists of a total 10 Clubs & 4 Committees. All of these Clubs, Committees are quite active in their fields, have brought laurels to the Institute and are growing at a good rate. Members of these Clubs & Committees are super dedicated towards their roles and are fully responsible for the growth and development of the Clubs over the years.
