About Student Gymkhana

Student Gymkhana -IIIT Vadadora was established in 2011 and since then it has been instrumental in the growth and development of the Institute and its students through its elected secretaries. Student Gymkhana-IIIT Vadadora consists of eight secretaries all organizing collective student activities in their respective spheres.
