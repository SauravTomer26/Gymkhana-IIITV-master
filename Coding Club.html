IIIT-V CC : The Coding Club

The Coding Club is a peer-learning community for developing skills in Programming and to improve the technical credibility of the students.

Description of the Club:

The Coding Club is a community of students who help organize and oversee various Programming related events in the institute. They conduct workshops, quizzes, talks, competitions and study-jams around the year in the institute and help build a stronger technical environment for the students.

About Student Gymkhana

IIIT Vadodara Student-Gymkhana aims at maintaining and providing good extra co-curricular activities to it's students ensuring a perfect balance between their academics & social life through various events conducted by its Clubs headed by different Committees. IIIT Vadodara Student Gymkhana consists of a total 10 Clubs & 4 Committees. All of these Clubs, Committees are quite active in their fields, have brought laurels to the Institute and are growing at a good rate. Members of these Clubs & Committees are super dedicated towards their roles and are fully responsible for the growth and development of the Clubs over the years.
